Sponsorship Options for INLG 2021

The 2021 International Natural Language Generation (INLG) conference will be hosted by the University of Aberdeen during the week of 20 Sep 2021. We are looking for companies to sponsor the event. There are three sponsorship levels: Bronze, Silver, and Gold.

INLG is the leading specialist conference for natural language generation and has been held regularly since the late 1980s. It is organized by the Special Interest Group in Generation (SIGGEN) of the Association for Computational Linguistics (ACL). It generally attracts around 200 attendees from universities and corporate research labs. INLG 2021 will primarily be online (virtual), but we will provide additional activities for participants who are able to physically come to Aberdeen.

For more information on INLG2021, see https://inlg2021.github.io/ .
For more information on sponsorship, please email Ehud Reiter, e.reiter@abdn.ac.uk


Bronze Sponsorship
Bronze sponsorship costs UK£500 and provides the following benefits.

  • 2 complimentary tickets to INLG2021.

  • Sponsor’s logo on INLG website and conference platform.

  • Space within online sponsor’s area.

  • Featured in emails and social media posts about sponsors.

    • Silver Sponsorship
    Silver sponsorship costs UK£1000 and provides the following benefits.

  • Opportunity to sponsor one of the INLG2021 sessions.

  • 4 complimentary tickets to INLG.

  • Sponsor’s logo on INLG website and conference platform.

  • Space within online sponsor’s area.

  • Featured in emails and social media posts about sponsors.

    • Gold Sponsorship
    Gold sponsorship costs UK£2000 and provides the following benefits.

  • Can sponsor an event for attendees who are physically in Aberdeen, such as breakfast, reception or happy hour, or excursion

  • Sponsor’s logo on INLG website and conference platform.

  • Space within online sponsor’s area.
