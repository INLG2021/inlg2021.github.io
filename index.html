The 14th International Conference on Natural Language Generation (INLG 2021)
organised by the Association for Computational Linguistics Special Interest Group on
Natural Language Generation (SIGGEN)
Aberdeen, United Kingdom 20 - 24 September, 2021.

Because of the Covid situation and associated restrictions on travel, INLG-2021 will take place online. All presentations of research work (oral and poster) will be online. We will have two events in Aberdeen, for people who live in Aberdeen or can travel there: a coffee morning at Arria on Thursday (23 Sep), and an excursion to a local castle on Friday (24 Sep).
